Some other design patterns are based on MVC, such as MVVM (Model-View-Viewmodel), MVP (Model-View-Presenter), 
and MVW (Model-View-Whatever).


1.Model: Manages data and business logic.
2.View: Handles layout and display.
3.Controller: Routes commands to the model and view parts.
